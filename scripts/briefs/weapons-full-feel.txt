--- RECOIL FEEL (client/graphics/firingFx.js) ---
Two independent layers per weapon:
 camKick = camera ROTATION recoil (degrees). Applied AFTER aim is read, removed before
   next read, so it is PROVABLY invisible to the shot ray (pure visual). Fields:
   pitch (per-shot climb), yawDrift (signed), yawJitter, climb/climbMax (sustained lean),
   heatBias (scales climb by predicted heat), tension/damping (return spring ~critically
   damped), fov (shotgun only, world-cam concussion punch).
 vmKick = viewmodel procedural kick: back/up/pitch/yaw impulse + spring tension/damping,
   optional pump (delayed 2nd rack), roll, per-shot variance (Vlambeer "vary everything").
